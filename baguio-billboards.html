Why Advertise in Baguio?

Baguio City is the Philippines' summer capital — a year-round tourist destination, major educational hub, and commercial center serving the Cordillera region. With consistent tourist influx and a dense student population, Baguio offers a unique OOH audience unlike any other Philippine city.

Baguio's compact urban layout means billboards achieve exceptional frequency — the city's limited road network concentrates traffic through a small number of high-visibility corridors. Tourist arrivals spike during summer (March–May) and the Panagbenga Festival (February), creating seasonal high-value windows for advertisers.

Key OOH Corridors in Baguio

Session Road

Baguio's iconic main street. High foot traffic, retail density, and tourist activity year-round.

Harrison Road

Key arterial road connecting Session Road to the southern parts of the city.

Magsaysay Avenue

Commercial corridor with strong local foot and vehicle traffic.

Naguilian Road

Main entry road from La Union. Reaches inter-city travelers entering Baguio from the west.

Kennon Road

Scenic mountain approach from Pangasinan. High tourist vehicle traffic.

Marcos Highway

Primary highway approach from Manila side. Reaches all travelers entering from the south.
