Why Advertise in Cebu?

Cebu is the Philippines' second-largest urban economy and its most important regional hub outside of Metro Manila. The Cebu OOH market offers strong reach at rates 30–50% lower than comparable Metro Manila placements — making it one of the best-value OOH markets in the country.

With a population of over 3 million in Metro Cebu and a booming BPO, tourism, and retail economy, Cebu delivers strong consumer reach at accessible rates. The city's premium corridors — SRP, the Airport Road, and the NRA — rival Metro Manila sites in audience quality while offering significantly better value.

Key OOH Corridors in Cebu

South Road Properties (SRP)

Cebu's premier reclamation area. High-income audience, major malls, strong traffic.

Mactan-Cebu Airport Road

Reaches tourists, business travelers, and OFWs daily. Premium audience.

North Reclamation Area (NRA)

Busy commercial strip connecting Cebu City to Mandaue. Strong retail audience.

Mandaue City

Industrial and commercial hub. Heavy B2B and mass market traffic.

Cebu IT Park / Lahug

BPO district. Young, educated, high-income professional audience.

Lapu-Lapu / Mactan

Growing island market. Tourist and residential growth driving new OOH demand.
