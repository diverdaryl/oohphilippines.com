Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?

Click the Subscribe button, enter your email, and scan the QR code with GCash, Maya, BPI, or any QR Ph-enabled banking app. Your account activates automatically after payment.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time and you won't be billed for the next period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via QR Ph — scan with GCash, Maya, BPI, UnionBank, and all major Philippine banking apps. Fast and secure.

Can I upgrade from Pro to Agency?

Absolutely. Just email us at info@oohphilippines.com and we'll handle the upgrade. It takes effect immediately with no downtime.

Is the Free plan really free forever?

Yes. You can search and browse all billboard sites for free. Paid plans unlock unlimited searches, full contact details, shortlisting, and more.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or startups?

Yes! We support the local advertising industry. Contact us at info@oohphilippines.com and we'll work something out.
