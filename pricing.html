Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?

Click the Subscribe button, enter your email, and scan the QR code with GCash, Maya, BPI, or any QR Ph-enabled banking app. Your account activates automatically after payment.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time and you won't be billed for the next period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via QR Ph — scan with GCash, Maya, BPI, UnionBank, and all major Philippine banking apps.

My billboard is not yet in the directory. Can I still get featured?

Yes! When you click "Get Featured", you can indicate that your billboard is not yet listed and fill in your billboard details. We'll add it to the directory and mark it as featured.

Can I upgrade from Pro to Agency?

Absolutely. Just email us at info@oohphilippines.com and we'll handle the upgrade. It takes effect immediately.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or startups?

Yes! Contact us at info@oohphilippines.com and we'll work something out.
