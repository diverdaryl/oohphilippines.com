About OOHPhilippines.com

Your trusted platform for Out-of-Home advertising in the Philippines

Who We Are

OOHPhilippines.com is the leading online platform dedicated to connecting advertisers, media practitioners, and billboard operators across the Philippines. We simplify the process of finding, comparing, and booking outdoor advertising spaces.

Our Mission

To make Out-of-Home advertising accessible, transparent, and efficient for everyone in the Philippines. We believe that finding the perfect billboard location should be as easy as searching online.

What We Offer

📍 Comprehensive Billboard Inventory

Browse hundreds of billboard locations across Metro Manila and provincial areas. Each listing includes exact locations, specifications, pricing, and direct contact information.

🎉 Exclusive Promotions

Access special deals and packages from top billboard operators. Save money while maximizing your campaign's reach.

🤝 Direct Connections

Connect directly with billboard operators and advertisers. No middlemen, no complications - just straightforward business relationships.

💡 Industry Resources

Stay updated with the latest trends, best practices, and opportunities in the Philippine OOH advertising industry.

Who Uses OOHPhilippines.com?

  • Advertisers & Brands - Find the perfect locations for your campaigns
  • Media Agencies - Research and plan OOH campaigns for clients
  • Billboard Operators - List your inventory and connect with advertisers
  • Marketing Professionals - Stay informed about the OOH landscape

Why Choose Us?

✓ Free to Use - Browse and search at no cost

✓ Real Locations - All listings include actual Google Street View

✓ Up-to-Date Information - Regularly updated inventory and pricing

✓ Direct Contact - Reach operators via email, phone, WhatsApp, or Viber

✓ Locally Focused - Built specifically for the Philippine market

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a platform that is:

  • Accurate and reliable

  • Continuously improving based on your feedback
