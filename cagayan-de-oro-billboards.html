Why Advertise in Cagayan de Oro?

Cagayan de Oro is Northern Mindanao's commercial and logistics hub — a rapidly growing city with a young population, expanding BPO sector, and strong retail market. CDO serves as the gateway to a wider regional market covering Northern Mindanao and the Caraga region.

CDO's position as Mindanao's logistics center means billboard audiences include not just local residents but traders, business travelers, and logistics professionals moving goods across the region. Combined with a young, aspirational consumer base and low billboard clutter, CDO offers strong OOH value.

Key OOH Corridors in Cagayan de Oro

Limketkai Drive

CDO's premier commercial corridor. SM CDO, Limketkai Center, and major retail hubs.

Divisoria Area

CDO's traditional commercial district. High pedestrian and vehicle traffic.

Macabalan Port Road

Kauswagan National Highway

Eastern bypass serving growing residential and commercial areas.

CDO Airport Road

Laguindingan Airport approach. Reaches travelers and business visitors.
